How you can help

by: D. G. Lopez

You are an ordinary citizen. And like me, you are disgusted at the fact that millions of people live in a hell they do not deserve. So here's a list of how you can help these people.

  1. Extend Financial Support

    One thing you can do is donate to charitable (and reputable) organizations that have actual programs. Before you do so, make sure to research more about the organizations you will give your money to in order to ensure the donations they receive are used in an efficient and transparent way.

  2. Volunteer Directly

    You can become part of the solution by being involved with initiatives involved in poverty alleviation.An example of this would be educational support services, wherein you can tutor children in poor families to help them succeed, both academically amnd personally, and this helps them realize their dreams.

  3. Educate The People

    Raising awareness about poverty can also help thos living in it. By doing so, other ordinary citizens like you are encouraged to help the poor. This helps alleviates the suffering caused by poverty.
