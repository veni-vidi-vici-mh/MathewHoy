About Us

Decagram Charity and Education Enterprises is a volunteer organization that is dedicated to charity for the poor, and raising awareness about poverty. Our organization has hundreds of branches in the Philippines, and is currently headquartered in the City of Manila.

This organization started in 2039 when its founder, Danilo G. Lopez, realized the need to address poverty within the Philippines, especially when he travelled to many slums across the country. He realized the need to educate people in his country about the poor, and also to help the poor themselves.

Currently, the vice chairman and co-founder(photo shown above) runs Decagram's official website. He has a master's degree in sociology and political science. He is also a member of the Philippine Social Science Council.
